About Me

I'm a Mechatronics Engineering student at the University of Guelph with a passion for bridging mechanical design with intelligent software. As a sub-team lead for the University of Guelph Robotics Team (UGRT), I specialize in CAD integration, UI panel casing, and rover frame alterations.

Outside of my formal studies, I'm a builder at heart. You'll often find me designing in SolidWorks, writing C++ for Arduino systems, or 3D printing custom parts on my Sovol SV07 Plus for personal projects like "Hank," a custom robot utilizing a dog-clutch knee transmission. I also hold an Advanced Remotely Piloted Aircraft certificate from Transport Canada. When I'm not bringing mechatronic concepts to life, I'm usually tracking down specific LEGO sets or cheering on the Edmonton Oilers.
